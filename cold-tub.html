Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the ArcticFlow cold plunge experience.

How does the ArcticFlow compare to traditional ice baths?
The ArcticFlow offers precise temperature control (±0.5°F), consistent cooling without ice, integrated sanitation, and energy-efficient operation. Traditional ice baths require constant monitoring, manual ice addition, and lack temperature consistency.
What is the installation process?
Our white-glove service includes delivery, assembly, and testing at your location. We require a standard electrical outlet and level surface. The entire process takes approximately 2-3 hours, including personalized training on operation and maintenance.
How much does it cost to operate?
With our advanced insulation and efficient compressor system, operating costs average $25-40 per month depending on local electricity rates and usage frequency. This is significantly lower than traditional chillers which can cost $80+ monthly.
What maintenance is required?
The integrated ozone system minimizes maintenance. We recommend checking water chemistry monthly and changing water every 4-6 months. The system includes self-diagnostic features and remote monitoring for proactive maintenance alerts.
Is financing available?
Yes, we offer 0% APR financing for qualified buyers with terms of 12, 24, or 36 months. Applications are processed quickly with instant decisions in most cases. Leasing options are also available for commercial installations.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with your ArcticFlow experience, we'll arrange pickup and provide a full refund. Installation fees are non-refundable once service is completed.
How long does delivery take?
Standard delivery is 2-4 weeks after order confirmation. Expedited shipping is available for an additional fee, reducing delivery time to 5-7 business days. International shipping times vary by destination.
Can the ArcticFlow be used outdoors?
Yes, the ArcticFlow is designed for both indoor and outdoor use. The marine-grade construction and advanced insulation maintain performance in temperatures from -20°F to 110°F. We recommend using the included cover when not in use.
